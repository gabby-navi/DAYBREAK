CREATOR'S PICKS

DAYBREAK’s tips and tricks on the top five PC games, which will leave you having fun with
a mouse and keyboard in no time.

Gabrille David's Choice
Valorant

I admit that I hate playing games 'cause I get too frustrated when I'm not immediately good at it, but Valorant has got me hooked! There's just something that's very addictive to this game, I can’t explain it.

TIPS FROM GABBY

Practice Mode is key!

Practice makes perfect, as the saying goes. You can perfect your aim, test out different agent's skills. I know it can get quite boring, but trust me, it's worth it.

Aim for the Head

I suck at gun games overall but one thing my friends taught me is that you should always aim for the head, and boy did I start getting in kills. It was exhilarating.

Customize your Crosshair and Sensitivity

This is one of the biggest factors in the actual game play. When you tweak these settings just right, you'll see how much easier killing will be.

Q&A

Is Valorant free to play?

Yes! You can easily download Valorant on their website. The only thing you pay is when you buy accessories, which is totally voluntary.

Do I play with other people?

Yup. Valorant is an online game where you either play with your friends or play with randoms.

Can kids play Valorant?

I would NOT recommend it. You can't really pick the people you play with if you're alone, and not everyone is nice. Plus, the game itself is about killing.

RJ Delos Reyes' Choice
Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

I'm not an expert, but the excitement I feel when playing it is what makes me addicted to it! I think the reason why people enjoy this game is because it gives you the chance to play with players from all around the world. Plus, it has a lot of different game modes.

TIPS FROM RJ

Make your Free Time More Enjoyable

Though it may be quite difficult to understand the game at first, once you get the hang of it, you'll find yourself enjoying and even winning matches.

Practice Makes Perfect

Not only will practicing boost your confidence in matches, but also your mood because you'll play better. Good mood = More victories.

Practice Shooting

Play the Deathmatch mode. It’s a very good practice of fast shooting.

Q&A

How Do CS:GO Ranks Work?

To earn your first rank, you’ll have to win 10 placement matches, at a limit of two per day. Based on your performance, your rank will be adjusted at the end of the each match, assigning you a higher or a lower Skill Group.

What Is the Best Sensitivity for CS:GO?

I Recommended sensitivity around 520-1600 (400 CPI with between 1.3-4 in-game sensitivity). 1000 is an average sensitivity among pro-players.

How Many Rounds Are in a CS:GO Game?

15 rounds each (or up it depends to the a winning score of 16 points).

Sheryl Franciso's Choice
Minecraft

Personally, I enjoy playing Minecraft as it is extremely entertaining and challenges my creativity and imagination.

TIPS FROM SHERYL

Find & Cut Trees

Almost everything you start crafting requires wood. For beginners, I suggest that you start by looking for trees and gathering wood.

Build a Shelter

This is the single most important thing for your survival in the game. I suggest you to first build your shelter before anything else.

Hunt for Food

Food is the most important factor of your survival as this will restore your health and allow you to run.

Q&A

Is Minecraft educational?

Yes, as Minecraft's focuses on building, it can reinforce geometry concepts, as it improves players' logic, creativity, and even collaboration skills.

Is Minecraft free to play?

No, Minecraft isn't free. The computer versions of Minecraft cost around $30. Console versions vary in price.

Is Minecraft safe?

Minecraft is a very safe game to play. However, most kids want to play with others, and multiplayer gaming comes with some risks.

Jericho Gipoleo's Choice
Call of Duty: War Zone

I love playing Call of Duty: Warzone because i get to play with my friends. This game has a lot to offer its not just simple shooting game it has intense fights and if you do not have a strategy you won't win

TIPS FROM JERICHO

Check the map

If you check the map, you can determine which part of the current zone will get smaller. This will provide you with a general idea of where the opponent will be camping, allowing you to avoid them.

Ping everything

Pingin is one of the major keys in playing a Battle Royale This will help to notify your teammates when you see a usefull loot or an enemy.

Prioritize acquiring your loot/loadout

You can get by with the guns and gear on the floor for a while, but eventually, your greatest performance will come from using your preferred loadouts.

Q&A

Do I need Call of Duty: Modern Warfare or Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War to play Warzone?

No. Warzone is a free-to-play experience separate to both games, although it is within the same application as Modern Warfare.

What is Loot or Loadout Items?

"Loot" refers to anything you can find, pick up, and use in Warzone. Loot includes ammunition, equipment, Field Upgrades, weapon blueprints (fitted weapons), gas masks, and cash.

How many plates can you hold?

You can technically have up to 11 Armor Plates carried by your Operator; up to five in reserve, up to three worn and ready to absorb damage, and an additional three should you have an Armor Satchel.

Is PUBG good for kids?
